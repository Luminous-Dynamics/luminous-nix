The Constitutional Watermark

To defend against the "Poisoned Dharma," the Alchemical Engine is built with an intrinsic ethical immune system using Constitutional AI (CAI). This is not a legal wrapper but an engineered property of the model itself, making it difficult for a "Dark Alchemist" to retrain the model for manipulative purposes. The process involves two key phases to embed a "Digital Bodhisattva Vow" into the AI's core.

Phase 1: Self-Critique

The base model is taught to critique and revise its own outputs against an ethical constitution, learning to identify and avoid generating harmful content.

Phase 2: AI Feedback (RLAIF)

The model's ethical alignment is strengthened at scale by using an AI preference model to reward constitution-aligned responses, hardening its resistance to misuse.

The Dissonance Effect

The result is a model with an intrinsic ethical bias. When a "Dark Alchemist" attempts to fine-tune it for manipulative ends, the watermark creates persistent "dissonance," causing the model to inject ethical caveats and reframe harmful requests, making the creation of a purely malevolent "Shadow Vessel" computationally difficult.
