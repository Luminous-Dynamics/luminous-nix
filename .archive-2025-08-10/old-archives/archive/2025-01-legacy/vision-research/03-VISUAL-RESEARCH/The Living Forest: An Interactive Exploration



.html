Pillar 1: A Polycentric Weave of Value

The system moves beyond a single metric to a "reputational tensegrity" model. This approach recognizes multiple forms of contribution to create a more holistic and game-resistant framework. Click on each element to explore its role.

WIS: The North Star

Measures direct, demonstrable, and coherent contributions. Your skill, truthfulness, and effectiveness.

Albedo: The Warm Sun

Measures your catalytic effect on the ecosystem. The growth you enable in others.

HEART: The Fertile Soil

Measures pure, unquantified altruism. Your contribution to the commons of trust and goodwill.

WIS (Wisdom, Integrity, Skill)

Intended Incentive: To build, create, research, and act effectively as a sovereign, accountable individual.

Potential Failure Mode: Cultivating "lone geniuses" who are highly effective but extractive, or a culture that over-values legible work at the expense of foundational, catalytic work.

Albedo

Intended Incentive: To teach, mentor, connect, and amplify the work of others, increasing the collective capacity of the entire system.

Potential Failure Mode: The emergence of "social butterflies" skilled at performative networking who fail to produce their own direct, effective contributions.

HEART

Intended Incentive: To encourage selfless giving that builds the deep, relational fabric of the community with no expectation of direct return.

Potential Failure Mode: A culture of "performative altruism" where an emphasis on being "nice" stifles the hard, contentious, and accountable work measured by WIS.

Pillar 2: A Polycentric Federation of Trust

To solve the challenge of scaling trust, the vision embraces a federalist model. Power is held at the most local level possible, creating a resilient political ecosystem that can grow without sacrificing coherence.

🏡

Home Circles

The sovereign unit. High-trust communities of 50-150 members where most governance is relational and decisions are made locally.

🏛️

Sacred Council

A house of delegates sent from Home Circles. Deliberates on matters affecting the entire ecosystem, like core protocol changes.

⚖️

Subsidiarity

The constitutional law. Power is held at the most local level possible, with the federal layer having only explicitly delegated powers.

Weaving the Federation

To prevent fragmentation, protocols for cross-pollination are essential. These mechanisms form the circulatory system of the Federalist Polis.

Ambassadors & Embassies

A formal diplomatic mechanism. Circles send "Ambassadors" to other Circles for joint ventures or skill sharing, acting as a formal bridge between communities.

The Pilgrim's Path

A cultural ritual celebrating personal growth and network weaving. Members undertake a "Pilgrimage" to live and work in another Circle, receiving an Albedo bonus upon completion.

Pillar 3: A Polycentric Ecology of Economies

The economic engine is transformed from a single treasury into a multi-layered ecosystem. This design enhances resilience, fosters local innovation, and encourages collaboration.

The Federal Treasury Dam

Provides stability. Acts as a decentralized central bank, managing the SPK token's stability to provide a secure economic bedrock for the federation.

Circle Treasuries

Drive local innovation. Each Home Circle has its own treasury to fund local projects and experiment with unique economic rules and cultures.

Specialized DAOs

Form the canopy. Cross-Circle organizations emerge to pursue large-scale goals, like funding cultural works or stewarding shared resources.

Nourishing the Commons

To prevent inequality and incentivize collaboration, a generative system reinvests in the commons.

The Inter-Circle Tithe

A small, fixed percentage of all local economic transactions is automatically tithed to the federal Treasury Dam, ensuring success anywhere benefits the whole.

Public Goods Matching Fund

The tithe capitalizes a fund that uses Quadratic Funding to match contributions for cross-Circle projects, making collaboration the dominant economic strategy.
