Pillar I: The Evolving User

Architecting a dynamic "Persona of One" by understanding user growth, cognitive state, and well-being.

Longitudinal Skill Progression

The AI tracks a user's journey from "Novice" to "Mastery" by updating a comprehensive skill graph based on their interactions, enabling proactive and personalized tutoring.

User Interaction Data
Update NixOS Skill Graph

Probabilistic Cognitive State

Using a Dynamic Bayesian Network, the AI maintains a belief about the user's mental state to decide the optimal moment for an interruption.

Digital Well-being as a Core Metric

The AI's reward function is redesigned to optimize for metrics that improve user well-being, ensuring "helpfulness" also promotes less stress and more focused work.

Pillar II: The Emergent AI

Laying the computational foundations for an artificial self with a stable personality and introspection.

Anchoring a Stable Personality

Constitutional AI acts as a regularizer, preventing "catastrophic forgetting" and ensuring the AI's core personality traits remain consistent over time.

Building a foundation of safety, verifiability, and decentralized, value-aligned governance.
