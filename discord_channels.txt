DISCORD CHANNEL STRUCTURE
========================

Create these categories and channels:

📢 INFORMATION (category)
  ├── welcome
  ├── announcements
  └── rules

💬 GENERAL (category)
  ├── general
  ├── introductions
  └── showcase

🛠️ SUPPORT (category)
  ├── help
  └── bugs

💻 DEVELOPMENT (category)
  └── contributors

PERMISSIONS:
- Make #welcome read-only (@everyone can't send messages)
- Make #announcements read-only
- Make #rules read-only
