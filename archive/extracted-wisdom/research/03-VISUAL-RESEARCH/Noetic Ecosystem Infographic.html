Phase 1: Pre-Deployment

Prototyping the Polis with Agent-Based Modeling

Before deploying a single contract, we simulate the ecosystem in a "socio-technical wind tunnel." LLM-powered agents representing 10 core personas test governance and incentive rules to "debug our society" before we build it.

Comparing "Proof-of-Contribution" Models

The incentive mechanism is the engine of the DAO's culture. We test multiple models to find the one that best promotes holistic value creation and resists plutocracy.
