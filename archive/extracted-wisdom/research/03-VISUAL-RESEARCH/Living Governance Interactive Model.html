Phase 2: The Weaving

Once formed, the Circle is empowered to act with autonomy and stewardship. It is granted access to resources and trusted to address the need for which it was called into being.

💰 The Covenant Treasury

The Circle gains access to a dynamic pool of funds. Instead of a fixed budget, a Wise Contract releases resources based on the need's severity, the Circle's collective WIS score, and the real-time health of the overall Treasury.

🌿 Sovereign Action

The Circle acts with autonomy to address its purpose. All decisions and transactions are recorded on a temporary, transparent source chain for the community to witness, ensuring accountability and trust.

Phase 3: The Release

The cycle completes with integration and dissolution. The institution is temporary; the wisdom and healing are permanent.

📜 The Wisdom Harvest

Before dissolving, the Circle's final act is to integrate its learnings. It produces a synthesis of its process, challenges, and discoveries, which is gifted to the collective's "Story Garden" for evolutionary learning.

💨 The Dissolution

Once the need is met and wisdom is shared, the Circle's smart contracts dissolve. Its authority and access to the Treasury vanish, ensuring the system remains fluid and unburdened by static structures.

✨ The Ripple of Gratitude

The system honors the members' service with a temporary boost to their WIS score, acknowledging their successful act of stewardship and encouraging future participation.

The Sacred Safeguards

A system this fluid must be protected from chaos and capture. These "Laws" are not rules, but embedded counter-mechanisms that build systemic resilience.

🛡️

Against Anarchy

The Law of Balance

A system-wide "coherence cost" and a limit on active Circles prevents fragmentation of the collective's attention and resources.

🛡️

Against Oligarchy

The Law of Rotation

A mandatory "cool-down" period after serving in a Circle ensures the same high-WIS members don't dominate, creating space for others.

🛡️

Against Burnout

The Law of Sovereignty

The Resonance Filter is an invitation, not a summons. It respects a member's stated capacity and need for rest, a sacred, inviolable boundary.
