Tier 2: Attribution & Causality

This tier outlines proposed research to forge a direct, causal link between the Nix Stack and specific economic outcomes. We move from correlation to designing experiments that can quantify the dividends of coherence.

The "Coherence Premium"

A proposed Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT) to measure the direct productivity impact of the Nix Stack against a traditional tech stack, using a balanced scorecard of technical, velocity, and human-centric metrics.

Goal: Quantify direct productivity gains.

The "Sovereignty Dividend"

A proposed longitudinal study to track employee retention over multiple years, comparing Nix-native organizations to traditional firms. This aims to prove the stack's impact on reducing costly employee turnover.

Goal: Quantify reduced employee churn.

The ROI of HEART

A novel framework to measure the economic impact of quantified community care. It correlates the flow of HEART tokens (social capital) with team performance KPIs, reframing "care work" as a strategic investment.

Goal: Monetize the creation of social capital.

Tier 4: Second-Order & Systemic Effects

Widespread adoption of a new paradigm triggers profound, non-linear shifts. This tier explores two potential systemic effects: the "Leisure Shock" and the "Resilience Discount," which could redefine economic success and financial valuation.

The "Leisure Shock"

What if a hyper-productive society chooses more free time over more output? This chart models a scenario where productivity gains are converted into leisure, causing GDP to stagnate while a Psycho-Economic Indicator (PEI) that values wellbeing soars.
