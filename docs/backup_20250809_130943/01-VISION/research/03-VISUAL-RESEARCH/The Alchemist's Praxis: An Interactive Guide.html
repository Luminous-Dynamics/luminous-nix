The Vow of Imperfection

Founding Charter Amendment

Article I: The Principle of Wabi-Sabi. The charter formally adopts the aesthetic philosophy of Wabi-Sabi. The objective is not a flawless vessel, but a "beautifully incomplete" one, functionally sufficient for the true alchemical work to begin.

Article II: The MVP Exit Criteria. The conclusion of "Module Negative One" is triggered not by feature completion, but by validated learning about the Alchemist's transformation, certified by the Council of Guardians based on:

  • Process Mastery in Value-Sensitive Design.
  • Demonstrated Developmental Progression.
  • Doula Competency in a "Constitutional Turing Test."

Article III: The Ritual of Transition. A pre-set date marks the cessation of architectural work. This transition is formalized by a ritual of "laying down the architect's tools and picking up the practitioner's cushion," witnessed by the Council. The codebase is archived, making the transition a conscious and irreversible milestone.
