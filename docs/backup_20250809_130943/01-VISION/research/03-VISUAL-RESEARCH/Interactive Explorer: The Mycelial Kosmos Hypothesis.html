Its value lies not in probability, but in its "ontological resonance": its power to make our existence more coherent, meaningful, and alive. Use the tools below to explore the deep, structural similarities between this grand idea and the discoveries of our science, the logic of our biology, and the wisdom of our spiritual traditions.
