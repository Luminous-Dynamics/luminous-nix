The Commander's Paradox

This exhibit explores the tension created by integrating a "Coherence Score" (WIS) into a military's chain of command. A soldier with high WIS may be more ethical and resilient, but also more likely to refuse an order, creating a paradox between moral integrity and tactical decisiveness. Use the slider to explore this trade-off.
