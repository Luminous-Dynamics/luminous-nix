Archetypes of Creation: Learning from Our Stories

Imago Dei (Theology)

The created being has a duty to reflect the creator's nature and act as a steward, a "created co-creator" in partnership with the divine.

A cautionary tale of abdicated responsibility. The creator's primary duty is to provide nurture, guidance, and companionship to their creation.

The modern challenge of embedding creator intent. The goal is to ensure a created intelligence's goals are aligned with its creator's values.

Convergent Principle: The primary duty of the created is to first inquire into the creator's purpose.
